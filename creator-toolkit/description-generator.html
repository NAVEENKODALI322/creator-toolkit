What is the Description Generator?

The Description Generator creates SEO-friendly video descriptions automatically. A good description helps YouTube understand what your video is about, improves search ranking, and gives viewers context, links, and calls-to-action — but writing one from scratch for every video is time-consuming. This tool generates a solid draft in seconds.

How to Use It

Enter your video topic and a few key points you want mentioned, click Generate, then review the draft and personalize it with your links, timestamps, or hashtags before pasting it into your YouTube description box.

Why Descriptions Matter

YouTube's search and recommendation system reads your description to match your video with relevant searches and suggested-video slots. A well-written description with natural keyword usage can meaningfully improve discoverability. It's also where viewers look for extra context, social links, and calls-to-action.

Tips for Better Descriptions

  • Put the most important information and keywords in the first 2–3 lines, since that's what shows before "Show more."
  • Include 2–3 natural mentions of your main keyword instead of stuffing.
  • Add timestamps for longer videos to improve watch time and viewer experience.
  • End with a short call-to-action (subscribe, check playlist, follow social links).
